E - waste

At NEWTECH VIDEO SYSTEMS PVT LTD we understand that our responsibility doesn’t end at selling you our products. NEWTECH VIDEO SYSTEMS PVT LTD has been working in the area of safe disposal of electronic waste. This thought is supported by our global policy, as well the legislation passed by the Ministry of Environment and Forests (MoEF) (called E-waste (Management & Handling) Rules, 2016), which came into effect from 1st Oct 2016.

NEWTECH VIDEO SYSTEMS PVT LTD. will seek shared responsibility and cooperation from customers in reducing the environmental impact of their products.

NEWTECH VIDEO SYSTEMS PVT LTD will comply with all the applicable laws related to e-waste management Rules 2016 .

As part of the e-waste recycling initiative, NEWTECH VIDEO SYSTEMS PVT LTD. has authorized an E-waste Recycling company, named E-Waste Recylers India, to collect, dismantle and recycle E-waste – collected from all over India. E-Waste Recylers India, with its patented technologies, with over 200+ patents filed, is one of the top ten global players. E-Waste Recylers India vision is to look at E-waste not just as waste, but as an important resource that can be made useful – instead of shunning it as a social and environmental burden with their fully automated facility for e-waste recycling.

For further information about e-waste recycling, and our e-waste recycling partner & their process, you may log on to E-wasterecyclers.com or write an email to E-wasterecyclers.com or Call on Toll Free: 1800-1234-155

How this channelization will work

If you want to handover your old product than you can call on our e-waste recycling partner’s Toll Free No. 1800 1025 679. Customer care representatives will explain you about the process of disposal and will share the nearest drop point location to drop e-waste and If any customer wants to handover the material from their door steps then we do send E-waste Recyclers India reverse logistic team to collect the items and channelize the same to our e-waste partner plant for final processing.
